Our commitment to protecting the data of the institutions and individuals we serve.

Institutional Data Protection

BFZ Inc. is committed to maintaining the highest levels of confidentiality and data security. This policy outlines our approach to data management across all global engagements.

1. Data Collection

We only collect data that is strictly necessary for the execution of our professional advisory services. This may include organizational data, public sector statistics, or representative contact information.

2. Data Security

All digital assets are stored in encrypted environments with multi-factor authentication and strict access controls. We perform regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.

3. Data Sharing

BFZ Inc. does not sell, trade, or share client data with third parties for commercial purposes. Any data sharing with project stakeholders is governed by strict institutional protocols and legal agreements.
