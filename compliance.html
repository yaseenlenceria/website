Commitment to Institutional Compliance

Our work is governed by the highest ethical standards and international regulatory frameworks.

Ethics First Governance

BFZ Inc. understands that as a partner to governments and NGOs, our accountability is to the public interest. We have implemented a rigorous compliance framework that ensures every project is delivered with absolute transparency and fiscal responsibility.

Anti-Bribery & Corruption

Zero-tolerance policy aligned with the UK Bribery Act and US Foreign Corrupt Practices Act (FCPA). All staff and local partners undergo mandatory annual training.

Data Privacy & Security

All client data is handled within ISO 27001 certified environments. We strictly adhere to GDPR and local data residency requirements for sovereign clients.

Conflict of Interest

Rigorous disclosure and mitigation protocols for all strategic advisory engagements to ensure the neutrality and objectivity of our recommendations.
