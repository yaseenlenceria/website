Historical Foundation

A Legacy of Global Trust

Founded in 1998, Bradaran Faiz Zad inc (BFZ Inc) was established with a singular vision: to provide high-level strategic support to the organisations that shape our world.

From national government departments to international non-governmental organisations (NGOs), we have spent over two decades building a reputation for technical precision, logistical excellence, and ethical integrity. Our growth is mirrored by the success of our clients, as we evolve our methodologies to meet the shifting demands of the global landscape.

Our multidisciplinary team comprises senior policy advisors, seasoned project managers, and technical specialists with deep experience in international development, public administration, and global supply chain management. We pride ourselves on being a "real" partner—one that brings tangible results and a verifiable track record to every engagement.
