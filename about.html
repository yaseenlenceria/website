Our Story

Company Profile & History

Bradaran Faiz Zad Inc. (BFZ Inc.) was founded in 2007 in Afghanistan with a mandate to support public institutions through reliable procurement, technical implementation, and operational excellence.

Today, headquartered in Canada, BFZ Inc. delivers its services in full alignment with Canadian public‑sector procurement standards, regulatory requirements, and transparency expectations.

Our operational history includes the successful delivery of major institutional projects in Afghanistan, including data centre development, IT equipment supply, enterprise‑level network implementation, and the procurement of a wide range of vehicles for governmental and non‑governmental organizations. This field experience has strengthened our capacity to manage complex projects, coordinate multi‑layered logistics, and deliver mission‑critical goods under demanding conditions.

Our multidisciplinary team consists of senior advisors, project managers, and technical specialists with expertise in institutional development, public administration, technology infrastructure, and supply‑chain management. BFZ Inc. is committed to providing measurable outcomes, on‑time delivery, and full compliance with public‑sector procurement standards across every engagement.
